TABITA FOUNDATION

Proclaiming the Word, Building Lives, Serving the Community.

Our Vision

To be a tapestry of hope where the vulnerable are clothed with dignity, the poor are empowered through grace, and every life is restored by the love of Christ.

Our Mission

To follow the example of Tabitha by providing practical relief to those in need, fostering a community of compassionate discipleship, and demonstrating the life-giving power of the Gospel through consistent acts of service and kindness

Our Activities

Sunday Worship

Experience a powerful time of praise, prayer, and biblical teaching every Sunday morning.

Youth Ministry

Empowering young people to lead and grow in their spiritual journey through weekly fellowship.

Outreach Programs

Taking the love of the foundation beyond the walls through local community service and charity.
