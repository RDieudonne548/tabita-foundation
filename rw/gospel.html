Ubutumwa Bwiza

Kugarura icyizere binyuze mu kwizera n'ibikorwa

"I Yopa habaga umwigishwa witwaga Tabita... yagiraga imirimo myiza myinshi, n'ibikorwa byo gufasha abakene."

— Ibyakozwe n'Intumwa 9:36

Umurage wa Tabita

Tabitha ntiyari umuboshyi gusa. Yari umwigishwa wagaragarizaga kwizera kwe mu bikorwa. Ubuzima bwe butwigisha ko Ubutumwa Bwiza butabwumva gusa, ahubwo bugaragara binyuze mu rukundo no gufasha abandi.

Ubutumwa bw'Icyizere

Ubutumwa Bwiza ni inkuru nziza yuko binyuze muri Yesu Kristo, duhabwa intangiriro nshya. Umuryango wacu ubaho kugira ngo ube urugero ruzima rw'ubwo buntu, ufasha abari mu kaga kandi ushyigikira abato kugira ngo bagendere mu mugambi Imana yabateganyirije.
