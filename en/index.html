TABITA FOUNDATION

Proclaiming the Word, Building Lives, Serving the Community.

Our Vision

To build a broad community where those overlooked by others,especially the sick, children, and people living with disabilities,are given value, love, and equal opportunities, so that they may become self-reliant and live a life of hope and dignity.

Our Mission

To transform the lives of the vulnerable by providing basic support, building their capacity, and helping them achieve sustainable development so they can have independent and hopeful lives

Our Activities

Sunday Worship

Experience a powerful time of praise, prayer, and biblical teaching every Sunday morning.

Youth Ministry

Empowering young people to lead and grow in their spiritual journey through weekly fellowship.

Outreach Programs

Taking the love of the foundation beyond the walls through local community service and charity.
