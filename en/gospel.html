The Gospel Message

Restoring Hope Through Faith and Action

"In Joppa there was a disciple named Tabitha; she was always doing good and helping the poor."

— Acts 9:36

The Legacy of Tabitha

Tabitha, also known as Dorcas, was more than just a seamstress. She was a disciple whose faith was expressed through her hands. Her life teaches us that the Gospel is not just heard, but seen through acts of compassion and service.

At Tabitha Foundation, we believe that every act of kindness is a reflection of God's love. Just as Tabitha was restored to life to continue her work, we aim to bring restoration and dignity to those in our community in Kigali.

A Message of Hope

The Gospel is the good news that through Jesus Christ, we are offered a new beginning. Our foundation exists to be a living example of this grace, clothing the vulnerable and empowering the next generation to walk in their God-given purpose.
